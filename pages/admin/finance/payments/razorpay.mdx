# Razorpay

### Step 1: Create a Razorpay account
Open your browser and go to [https://dashboard.razorpay.com/signup](https://dashboard.razorpay.com/signup). Fill out the form and click `Verify & Submit`.

### Step 2: Get your API keys
After you've created your account, you'll need to get your API keys. Go to [https://dashboard.razorpay.com/app/website-app-settings/api-keys](https://dashboard.razorpay.com/app/website-app-settings/api-keys) <br/>
Now, copy `key_id` and click `Regenerate Key` button to get your `secret key`.

### Step 3: Configure your API keys
Go to your website's admin panel and navigate to `Finance Management` > `Gateways`. Find `Razorpay` and click the `edit button`, or go to [https://yourdomain.com/admin/finance/gateways](https://yourdomain.com/admin/finance/gateways). <br/>
Paste your `API Key` and `API Secret`. <br/>
Click `Update Changes` button to save your changes.
