# Razorpay

### Step 1: Create a Razorpay account
Open your browser and go to [https://dashboard.razorpay.com/signup](https://dashboard.razorpay.com/signup). Fill out the form and click "Verify & Submit".
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Web-capture_5-3-2023_1378_easy.razorpay.com.jpeg)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic64043fdfab4931925151950)

### Step 2: Get your API keys
After you've created your account, you'll need to get your API keys. Go to [https://dashboard.razorpay.com/app/website-app-settings/api-keys](https://dashboard.razorpay.com/app/website-app-settings/api-keys) <br/>
Now, copy key_id and click "Regenerate Key" button to get your secret key.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Web-capture_5-3-2023_131317_dashboard.razorpay.com.jpeg)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640441198713b549056968)

### Step 3: Configure your API keys
Go to your website's admin panel and go to **Finance Management > Gateways. Find Razorpay and click to edit button**. or go to [https://yourdomain.com/admin/finance/gateways](https://yourdomain.com/admin/finance/gateways)
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-05-12-39-56.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640441524313e1839866040)
<br/>
Paste your **API Key** and **API Secret**. <br/>
Click "Update Changes" button to save your changes.

[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-05-12-39-58.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640441bcb6ba61714154204)
