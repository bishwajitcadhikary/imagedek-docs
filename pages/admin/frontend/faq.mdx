# FAQ Manager

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/faqs](https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/faqs).

Here you can manage the FAQs on your website. This includes creating new FAQs, editing existing ones, and deleting FAQs you no longer need.

### Features
1. **FAQ List**: View all the FAQs currently available on your website.
2. **Create a New FAQ**: Add new FAQs with custom content and settings.
3. **Edit an FAQ**: Modify the content or settings of an existing FAQ.
4. **Delete an FAQ**: Remove FAQs that are no longer required.

### How to Use
1. Navigate to the `FAQ Manager` page in your admin panel.
2. Use the `FAQ List` to view and select the FAQ you want to manage.
3. Click the `Create`, `Edit`, or `Delete` button based on your desired action.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process.

Note: Always preview your changes before saving to ensure the FAQ appears as intended.
