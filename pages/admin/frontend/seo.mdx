# SEO Manager

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/seo](https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/seo)
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-19-17-46-05.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic6416f6f75c856578804151)

You can see the SEO Manager page. Here you can manage the SEO settings for your website. You can also manage the SEO settings for the pages of your website. You can also manage the SEO settings for the blog posts of your website. You can also manage the SEO settings for the products of your website.
