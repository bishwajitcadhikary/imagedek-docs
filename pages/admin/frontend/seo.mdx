# SEO Manager

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/seo](https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/seo)

Here you can manage the `SEO settings` for your website. This includes optimizing the visibility of your website, pages, blog posts, and products in search engines.

### Features
1. **Website SEO**: Configure global SEO settings for your website, such as meta tags, titles, and descriptions.
2. **Page SEO**: Manage SEO settings for individual pages, including keywords and descriptions.
3. **Blog Post SEO**: Optimize blog posts for search engines by adding relevant meta information.
4. **Product SEO**: Improve the visibility of your products by managing their SEO settings.

### How to Use
1. Navigate to the `SEO Manager` page in your admin panel.
2. Select the section you want to manage (e.g., `Website`, `Pages`, or `Blog Posts`).
3. Update the fields with the desired SEO information.
4. Click the `Save Changes` button to apply your updates.

Note: Regularly updating your SEO settings can help improve your website's search engine ranking.
