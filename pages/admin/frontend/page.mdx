# Page Manager

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/pages](https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/pages).

Here you can manage the pages on your website. This includes creating new pages, editing existing ones, and deleting pages you no longer need.

### Features
1. **Pages List**: View all the pages currently available on your website.
2. **Create a New Page**: Add new pages to your website with custom content and settings.
3. **Edit a Page**: Modify the content, layout, or settings of an existing page.
4. **Delete a Page**: Remove pages that are no longer required.

### How to Use
1. Navigate to the `Page Manager` page in your admin panel.
2. Use the `Pages List` to view and select the page you want to manage.
3. Click the `Create`, `Edit`, or `Delete` button based on your desired action.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process.

Note: Always preview your changes before saving to ensure the page appears as intended.
