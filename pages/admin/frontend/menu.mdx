# Menu Builder

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/menus](https://yourdomain.com/admin/frontend/menus)
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-13-06-06-13.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640e68ff2de851990342816)

You can drag and drop the menu items to change the order of the menu items. You can also add new menu items by clicking on the **Magic Stick Icon** button.

[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-13-06-05-06.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640e68bb174f949675816)
