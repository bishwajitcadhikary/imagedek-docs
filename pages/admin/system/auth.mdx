# Authentication

## Social Authentication
Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social](https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social) <br/>
Here you can enable or disable the social authentication. <br/>
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Web-capture_5-3-2023_151949_aiimage.abndevs.net.jpeg)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic64045eca52e581251749623)

### Enable Social Login
Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social](https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social) <br/>
Find the **Enable Social Login** option and click on the **Dropdown** and Select **Enable** button.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-05-14-25-60.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic64045e3912661977269049)

### Enable Facebook Login
Here you can enable or disable the Facebook authentication. <br/>

#### Step 1: Create a Facebook App
Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://developers.facebook.com/docs/facebook-login/guides/advanced/manual-flow/](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/facebook-login/guides/advanced/manual-flow/) . Follow the steps to create a Facebook App. <br/>
Note: You can also use the existing Facebook App. <br/>
Now you will get the **App ID** and **App Secret**. <br/>
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-05-14-25-61.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640460157d8de2119337051)

#### Step 2: Enable Facebook Login
Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social](https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social) <br/>
Find the **Enable Facebook Login** option and click on the **Enable Facebook Login** checkbox. <br/>
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-05-14-25-62_1.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640460b8a8bbc891613904)

### Enable Twitter Login
Here you can enable or disable the Twitter authentication. <br/>

#### Step 1: Create a Twitter App
Go to Twitter Developer App to click here: https://developer.twitter.com/en/portal/projects-and-apps
1. Click on the **Create App** button.
2. Fill the required details.
3. Click on the **Create** button.
4. Head over to the API Keys section; you can get your Consumer Key and Consumer secret keys from there.
5. Copy the consumer key (API key) and consumer secret from the screen into the laravel application.

Callback URL:
```
https://yourdomain.com/oauth/twitter/callback
```

#### Step 2: Enable Twitter Login
Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social](https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/social) <br/>

Find the **Enable Twitter Login** option and click on the **Enable Twitter Login** checkbox. <br/>
