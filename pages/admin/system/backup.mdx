# Backups Manager

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/backup](https://yourdomain.com/admin/system/backup).

Here you can manage backups for your website. This includes creating new backups, downloading existing ones, and restoring backups.

### Features
1. **Backups List**: View all the backups currently available, including their creation date and size.
2. **Create a New Backup**: Generate a new backup of your database, files, or both.
3. **Download a Backup**: Save a backup locally to your system.
4. **Restore a Backup**: Restore your website from an existing backup.

### How to Use
1. Navigate to the `Backups Manager` page in your admin panel.
2. Use the `Backups List` to view and select the backup you want to manage.
3. Click the `Create Backup`, `Download`, or `Restore` button based on your desired action.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process.

Note: Always verify the integrity of your backups after creating or restoring them to ensure your data is safe.
