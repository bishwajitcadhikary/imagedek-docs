# Support Tickets

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/support-tickets](https://yourdomain.com/admin/support-tickets) .

Here you can see all the support tickets that have been submitted by your customers. You can also create a new ticket by clicking on the “New Ticket” button.
