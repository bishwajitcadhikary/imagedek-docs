# Support Tickets

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/support-tickets](https://yourdomain.com/admin/support-tickets).

Here you can manage the support tickets submitted by your customers. This includes viewing, creating, and responding to tickets.

### Features
1. **Support Ticket List**: View all the support tickets submitted by customers.
2. **Create a New Support Ticket**: Add new tickets to address customer issues.
3. **View a Support Ticket**: Review the details of a specific ticket and respond to it.

### How to Use
1. Navigate to the `Support Tickets` page in your admin panel.
2. Use the `Support Ticket List` to view and select the ticket you want to manage.
3. Click the `New Ticket` button to create a new ticket or select an existing ticket to view and respond.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process.

Note: Always ensure that responses to tickets are clear and address the customer's concerns effectively.
