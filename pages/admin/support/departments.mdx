# Support Tickets

Open your browser and go to the following URL: [https://yourdomain.com/admin/support-departments](https://yourdomain.com/admin/support-departments).

Here you can manage support departments on your website. This includes creating new departments, editing existing ones, and deleting departments you no longer need.

### Features
1. **Support Department List**: View all the support departments currently available on your website.
2. **Create a New Support Department**: Add new support departments with custom settings.
3. **Edit a Support Department**: Modify the settings of an existing support department.
4. **Delete a Support Department**: Remove support departments that are no longer required.

### How to Use
1. Navigate to the `Support Tickets` page in your admin panel.
2. Use the `Support Department List` to view and select the department you want to manage.
3. Click the `Create`, `Edit`, or `Delete` button based on your desired action.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process.

Note: Always preview your changes before saving to ensure the department settings are correct.
