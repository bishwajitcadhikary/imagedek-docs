# Registration

Open your browser and visit the following URL:

[https://yourdomain.com/register](https://yourdomain.com/register)

### Step 1: Create an account
Fill in the form and click on the "Register" button.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-13-06-18-52.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640e6c4fae89c1126302868)

After that, you will receive an email with a link to verify your email address.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-13-06-19-40.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic640e6c253f0c21501237967)

### Step 2: Verify your email

Click on the **Verify Email Address** button or copy the link and paste it in your browser.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-05-16-24-51.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic64046e954ac15616087522)
