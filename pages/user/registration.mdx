# Registration

Open your browser and visit the following URL:

[https://yourdomain.com/register](https://yourdomain.com/register)

### Step 1: Create an account
Fill in the form and click on the "Register" button.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-19-18-37-39.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic641702cc34997509253704)

After that, you will receive an email with a link to verify your email address.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-19-18-39-14.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic641702cc34997509253704)

### Step 2: Verify your email

Click on the **Verify Email Address** button or copy the link and paste it in your browser.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-19-18-39-15.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic641702cc34997509253704)
