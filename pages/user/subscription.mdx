## Get subscription
### Step 1: Choose subscription
Open your browser and visit [https://yourdomain.com/pricing](https://yourdomain.com/pricing) and choose the subscription you want to buy.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-19-18-49-28.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic641705014a70f553948905)

### Step 2: Choose payment method
Choose the payment method you want to use to pay for the subscription. Now click on the "Checkout Now" button.
[![image](https://www.linkpicture.com/q/Screenshot-from-2023-03-19-18-49-38.png)](https://www.linkpicture.com/view.php?img=LPic641705014a70f553948905)
